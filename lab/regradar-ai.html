In Planning · AI / LLM

RegRadar AI

An AI compliance intelligence system for Indian businesses. Continuously monitors government websites, official gazettes, and regulatory circulars across thousands of rules — then delivers targeted, plain-language summaries directly to CAs, compliance officers, and SME owners before the deadline, not after the penalty.

"Never miss a regulatory change again."

The problem

Indian compliance is a moving target — and most businesses are blind to it

India operates across more than 1,500 compliance requirements spanning central, state, and sector-level regulatory bodies. GST rules alone change almost every month. SEBI, RBI, MCA, labour laws, environmental clearances, and sector-specific regulations each publish updates through separate government portals, official gazettes, and sector circulars — with no consolidated feed and no alert system.

Compliance officers and chartered accountants currently monitor this manually — checking over a hundred government websites and gazette portals daily just to stay current. Despite this effort, critical changes are still missed. Smaller businesses have no compliance team at all. They typically find out about regulatory changes after a penalty notice arrives — too late to avoid the fine, and sometimes too late to avoid legal consequences.

The solution

AI that monitors every regulation so your team does not have to

01 Monitor

AI agents continuously scrape and index updates from government websites, gazettes, ministry circulars, and regulatory body announcements — across all relevant jurisdictions and sectors.

02 Analyse

LLM processing extracts what changed, who it applies to, what the deadline is, and what action is required — before pushing it as a structured alert rather than raw document text.

03 Filter

Alerts are personalised to the business profile — sector, state, turnover range, applicable tax regime, and current compliance obligations. Irrelevant updates are filtered out.

04 Act

Recipients get a clear, plain-language summary: what changed, why it matters to them, what they need to do, and by when. Deadline tracking is built in, with escalating reminders as due dates approach.

Key capabilities

What the product will do

Regulatory monitoring agents

Automated AI agents that crawl the full landscape of Indian regulatory sources — CBIC, SEBI, RBI, MCA, FSSAI, state GST portals, and hundreds more — at regular intervals.

LLM-powered plain-language extraction

Converts dense government language — circular PDFs, gazette notifications, and press releases — into concise, actionable summaries that a non-legal reader can understand and act on.

Business profile matching

Each business configures its profile: industry, state, GST category, applicable labour laws, current licences. Alerts are filtered and ranked by relevance to that specific profile.

Deadline calendar

Every alert generates a deadline entry. The compliance calendar shows everything due in the next 7, 30, and 90 days — with escalating reminders and owner assignment for teams.

CA and multi-client mode

Chartered accountants managing multiple clients get a unified dashboard — see which clients are affected by each new regulation and push alerts directly to the relevant client contacts.

Audit trail

Every alert viewed, every action marked complete, every deadline acknowledged is logged — creating a verifiable compliance history that can be produced during regulatory audits.

Who it is for

Target users

The first target is chartered accountants and compliance officers who spend significant daily time monitoring regulatory sources manually and carry professional liability for missing a change. The second is Indian SMEs and startups — particularly in fintech, manufacturing, and food — who have no in-house compliance team and currently rely on WhatsApp groups and word-of-mouth to stay current. SaaS businesses with multi-state operations are a strong secondary market, given the complexity of navigating overlapping central and state-level requirements.
